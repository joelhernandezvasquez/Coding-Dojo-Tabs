We Believe in
Human Potential

Coding Dojo was founded on the belief that anyone can learn to code.

A career in tech should be available to anyone, regardless of age, background, gender or financial status. We don’t dismiss beginners or kick out those who struggle. Instead, we welcome students of all experience levels.
